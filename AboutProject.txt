- App to remember about the books you read
Based on (Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rvey9g0VgY0&ab_channel=MattD%27Avella)

- one of the handy way to remember the book you read is to highlight, take note, sort them under catergory and revisit them.

## App features

1. Read a book, Highlight the parts that interests you. Bookmark the page.Finish the book.

2. Come to the app, set the title and Author of the book. Transfer notes. 

3. categorize the notes into a bucket, Theme, whichever works for you on the back of the card.

4. filter through the category.

5. Reminder to read and practice what you learned.
